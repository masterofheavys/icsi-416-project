The King of Fighters XV is the latest installment in the KOF series. Announced at EVO 2019, the game was revealed in January 2021. KOF XV was released worldwide on February 17, 2022. The base roster includes 39 characters with 13 DLCs added over the course of the game's first year:

Team Garou was released on March 17th, 2022
Omega Rugal was released on April 14th, 2022
Team South Town was released on May 16th, 2022
Team Awakened Orochi was released on August 8th, 2022
Team Samurai was released on October 4th, 2022
Shingo Yabuki was released on January 17th, 2023
Kim Kaphwan was released on April 4th, 2023
Sylvie Paula Paula was released on May 16th, 2023
Goenitz was released on June 20th, 2023
Najd was released on August 8th, 2023
Duo Lon was released on September 12th, 2023
Hinako Shijo was released November 14th, 2023.
